Gambits

Disarm(Def): Target loses weapon, ally gains 1 Power per success.

Distract(2): May use Precense or Performance, decrease Defense by 1 per success.

Ensnare(3): Target cannot do Movement actions, lasts 1 + Extra successes rounds. The target may break free with a successful Close Combat roll vs extra Successes.

Knockback(4): Move target 1 range back, -1 Defense per success until end of round

Knockdown(4):Knock target prone

Pilfer(3): Roll Attribute + Stealth to steal and item, Give ally 1 power per extra success.

Pull(4): Move target 1 range band closser, -1 Defense per extra success for the round.

Reveal Weakness(3): May be used with craft or war. Roll an attack vs defense and the target has -2 soak for rounds equal to the attacks extra successes. If the target has 6+ soak their soak is halved instead

Unhorse(5): Knock horse from mount. Target is either knocked prone or Decrease his soak by 1 for each extra success for 1 round.
