Characters

-Getimian pool bonuses to attributes are calculated automatically

-Augment attribute (gear symbol next to attribute name) is calculated automatically for Alchemicals

-Targeting tokens (Double right click for players) will automatically set defense/resolve/soak values for social influence and attacks

-Onslaught penalties to hardness are auto calculated on withering attacks and removed on decisive attack.

-Spending motes will automatically raise anima. Increasing committed motes will spend motes (doesn't work for getimians)

-Build Power and Focus Will actions will automatically add power and will to the correct pools.

-The "Spend Charm" icon on charms will deduct the costs from the appropriate pools. Will increase committed by the ammount listed

NPCs

-Drill bonus to damage/accuracy and size bonus to Overwhelming is calculated automatically for Battlegroups
