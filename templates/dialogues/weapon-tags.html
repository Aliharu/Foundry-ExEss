Artifact: +1 to accuracy or damage.

Balanced: +1 Overwhelming.

Chopping: Enables chopping attacks (Wielder suffers -1 defense, gain +3 bonus dice on withering or -2 Enemy Hardness on attack)

Concealable: Weapon can be concealed with a diff 1 Stealth roll.

Defensive: Increase defense when Defending Other or Full Defense by 1.

Disarming: The melee weapon reduces the Power cost by one for disarm gambits.

Flame: Set things on fire, Deal +1 damage at close range.

Flexible: Ignores bonus from Full Defense. Flexible weapons reduce the cost of the ensnare gambit by one to a minimum of one.

Improvised: -2 Accuracy.

Melee: This weapon uses the Close Combat Ability to make attacks.

Mounted: The ranged weapon can be used while mounted on a steed.

Natural: Cannot be lost or stolen.

Worn: Increase the cost of the Disarm gambit by one.

Off-Hand: Has no accuracy/bonus, Attacks only suffer -1 penalty for making flurries.

One-Handed: Can be used one handed.

Paired: Withering attacks gain +1 power.

Piercing: Can make piercing attacks (Wielder suffers -1 defense, Reduce enemies soak by 2).

Pulling: Can make ranged attacks with Physique, Allow use to use the Pull gambit as a ranged attack.

Powerful: Powerful ranged weapons deal damage like a heavy melee weapon at close range.

Ranged: Uses Ranged Combat. Attacking a target in a closer range band than the listed range gives +1 Accuracy.

Reaching: Remove penalty for Enormous size and attacking mounted opponents.

Shield: User may flurry full defense actions. Shields have -1 Damage (Min 0).

Smashing: Reduce the cost of Knockback and Knockdown gambits by 1 (Min 1).

Thrown: Can be used with either ranged or close combat. Attacking a target in a closer Range band than the listed range grants +3 Accuracy.

Two-Handed: Wielded with 2 hands, Two-handed weapons have +1 Damage.
